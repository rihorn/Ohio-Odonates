The Ohio Odonata Society is collaborating with the Ohio Division of Wildlife to update the original 1991-2001 odonate survey. The value of species richness is normalized using the actual sampling effort (i.e., the values displayed show the total number of species as a percentage based on sampling effort). The updated survey will include:
OOS = Ohio Odonate Society
+ = OOS + iNaturalist
